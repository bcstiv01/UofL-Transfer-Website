ULtra Program

University of Louisville transfer (ULtra) offers services for students who aspire to earn their Bachelor’s degree from UofL but would like to start at Jefferson Community and Technical College (Jefferson). ULtra has an office on the Jefferson College Downtown and supports students while at Jefferson and through the transfer process.

Transfer Degree Pathways

When a student transfers to University of Louisville, the student’s official transcript will certify if the student is fully general education certified. If so, the student will not need to take any additional general education courses at UofL. The student is guaranteed that all the KCTCS courses indicated in the pathway will be accepted at UofL and that the courses will count toward completion of a particular major.

Adult Students

The University of Louisville classifies adult students as anyone who is 22 years of age or older. While you will go through a similar process as those younger than 22, you may have different needs and lifestyles that the university will take into consideration.Adult Students also include Adult Returning Students, Project Graduate and Senior Citizen Students.

Veteran & Active Duty Services

Our mission is to smooth the transition from military life to student life by devoting individualized support to veteran students and their families in providing a broad range of services, coordinating with VA, university departments, and other community organizations.Our Staff are capable of assisting our Veterans in their transfer process to UofL.
